About Me

Hello, I'm Virat Kohli, an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, cricket has been my passion from a young age.

I made my debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008, and since then, it has been an incredible journey filled with achievements, challenges, and memorable moments. As a right-handed top-orderNo.3 batsman, I've had the honor of leading the Indian team and contributing to numerous victories across formats.

Off the field, I'm fullydedicated to fitness, and my foundation works towards various social causes. This website serves as a platform to connect with my fans, share my experiences, and provide insights into my cricketing journey.
